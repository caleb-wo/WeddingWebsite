Our Story

We met at BYU–Idaho when our apartments were put in a church group together. We started spending time together—usually at his place playing ping pong and pool. He thought I was beautiful and someone worth the effort. I thought he was funny, confident, and yes… very handsome. I was shy at first, but he made me feel safe and seen, and over time, we grew closer. He loved my smile and quiet strength, and I admired the way he carried himself and made everyone laugh, although we grew to love each other on a much deeper level later on. A couple of months later, he asked me out—and from that point on, we’ve been inseparable!
